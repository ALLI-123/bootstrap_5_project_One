Frequently Asked Questions

The course is designed to be completed over a span of 7 months with a commitment of approximately 5-7 hours a week. Each week include video lectures, reading materials, and assignments that help reinforce the concepts learned. While the course is self-paced, we encourage participants to stay on track with the weekly schedule to maximize their learning experience

By the end of the course, you will have the skills to build several web applications. Examples are: a personal portfolio website that showcases your projects and skills, a "dynamic e-commerce platform" with user authentication and payment integration, and a "real-time chat appplication" using webSockets. Additionally, you will learn to create a "task management tool" similar to Trello using ReactJS for the front-end, NodeJS and ExpressJS for the Backend, and MongoDB as the database. You may also build small applications that integrate with third-party APIs, such as "weather dashboards" that fetch real-time data from a weather API. The final project would be a comprehensive application that incorporates all these skills, serving as a capstone to demonstrate your proficiency and creativity, providing you with a portfolio to show potential employees or clients.

Yes, upon successful completion of the course and all of its assessments, you will receive a certificate of completion This certificate is a great way to showcase your newly acquired skills on your resume, Linkedin profile, or personal website. It is important to note that the certificate is not an official accreditation but serves as a recognition of your hard work and dedication in completing the course.

The course is curated for beginners, so no prior programming experience is required. However, a basic understanding of how to use a computer and navigate the internet is helpful. We also recommend having a good grasp of problem-solving skills and logical thinking. All necessary software tools and resources will be provided, and you will be guided step-by-step through the set-up and use of each one.

Throughout the course, you will have access to a range of support options. These include: a dedicated course forum where you can ask questions and get help from instructors and fellow students, weekly live Q & A sessions with the course instructor, and 24/7 access to all course materials. If you encounter any technical issues or have questions about the course content, our support team is available to assist you via email or chat.
